About Case

Craftsman. Builder.
Father of the Workshop.

Case Vollink has been building things with his hands his entire life. Based in Texas, he has spent years constructing playsets, sheds, and custom homes for families across the DFW Metroplex — gaining a reputation for showing up, doing it right, and leaving people with something that lasts.

Now Case is channeling that same craft into his own workshop — turning raw lumber into furniture, built-ins, and one-of-a-kind pieces that make a room. If you can imagine it, he can build it.
